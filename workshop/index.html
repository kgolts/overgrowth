Friends! For the past two years we have been exploring beauty through Overgrowth and the world we’ve been creating is so close to becoming a reality. But before reach our final goal, we want to do something really special by bringing you into the project through an exclusive workshop!

This August, in a beautiful location on the coast of the Pacific Northwest, we’ll spend three days and three nights fully immersed in the Overgrowth world and each day we'll be taking you on a step-by-step tour of how we do what we do—no questions will be off limits.

We’ll be teaching you everything from constructing scenes, understanding light, botanical and floral design and model direction to color theory, cameras and film. Food, drinks and lodging are also included!

On top of all this, master photographer and friend Ryan Muirhead will be guest speaking during the weekend and will be there all three days providing instruction.
