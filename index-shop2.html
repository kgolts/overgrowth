Overgrowth

Overgrowth is the first of Ransom Limited’s sponsored Editions series and is the first joint project between Ransom Limited and Erba Studio.

Ransom Limited Editions is intended to be an annual collaboration with jury-selected artists and photographers, the aim of which is to and promote beautiful work through publication, exhibition, and online exposure.

Ransom Limited is also a new photographic cooperative based in Portland, OR. Founded in 2013 by brothers Parker and James Fitzgerald, Ransom photographs have been published and licensed worldwide.

Erba Studio is the work of Riley Messina. Based out of Portland, OR, she is known internationally for the cohesive simplicity and balance in her aesthetic. As a floral designer and stylist, Riley has worked throughout the United States and abroad.
